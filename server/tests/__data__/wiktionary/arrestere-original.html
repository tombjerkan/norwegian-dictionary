Dutch[edit]

Verb[edit]

arrestere

  1. (archaic) singular present subjunctive of arresteren

Anagrams[edit]

Norwegian Bokmål[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Old French arester, arrester.

Verb[edit]

arrestere (imperative arrester, present tense arresterer, simple past arresterte, past participle arrestert, present participle arresterende)

  1. to arrest

Synonyms[edit]

  • pågripe

Related terms[edit]

  • arrest
  • arrestasjon
